# 04-ladenheim / 03 — Reproduce 62 realizability-set equivalence classes

Status: `todo`

## Goal

Classify the 108-network Ladenheim catalogue into the **62 realizability-set
equivalence classes** reported by Morelli and Smith.

## Correct interpretation

The 62 classes are defined by equality of realizability sets as positive
component values vary. They are not simply a third topology count and are not
expected to follow from graph canonicalization alone.

Keep these objects separate:

- 108 catalogue members;
- 35 group-action orbits;
- 24 subfamilies;
- 62 realizability-set equivalence classes.

## Done means

- The 108-network catalogue has already been reproduced.
- The classification method is documented and tied to realizability-set equality.
- Algebraic network-analysis requirements and assumptions are explicit.
- Tests/fixtures demonstrate the reported 62 classes without discarding the 108
  individual catalogue members.
