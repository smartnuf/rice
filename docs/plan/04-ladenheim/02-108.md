# 04-ladenheim / 02 — Reproduce the 108-network Ladenheim catalogue

Status: `todo`

## Goal

Reproduce the **108-network canonical set** (the Ladenheim catalogue) as a stage
after the 148 essentially distinct networks have been independently reproduced.

## Correct interpretation

The transition from 148 to 108 removes forty networks using additional
electrical-network transformations and realizability arguments. It must not be
hidden inside the same structural signature used to obtain 148.

Categories to document and test:

- eight four-resistor/one-reactive networks with bilinear impedances realizable
  by simpler networks;
- four four-element networks reducible by a Zobel transformation to
  three-element networks;
- twenty five-element series-parallel networks reducible by a Zobel
  transformation to networks with four or fewer elements;
- eight O/O-dual, bridge, Cauer-Foster, regularity, and Y-Delta-related cases
  whose realizable impedances can be obtained with fewer elements.

## Done means

- The 148 stage already exists and is tested.
- The forty removals are implemented as a separately documented stage.
- Each removal category has fixtures or tests.
- The acceptance target is 108 catalogue members.
- The 108-network catalogue is not conflated with the 62 realizability-set
  equivalence classes, 35 group-action orbits, or 24 subfamilies.
