# 04-ladenheim / 02 — Compare with canonical 108-network Ladenheim catalogue

Status: `todo`

## Goal

Starting from the reproduced 148 structural catalogue, compare repository
classes with the known 108-network Ladenheim catalogue.

## Tasks

- Define the admissible star-delta contract before applying Y-to-delta or
  delta-to-Y moves.
- Apply the star-delta augmentation to the RICE local series/parallel track and
  to the colour-preserving 2-isomorphism track.
- Compare the resulting classes with the 108 catalogue.
- Identify any additional transformations, rejection criteria, or realizability
  arguments needed if star-delta alone does not account for the forty changes
  from 148 to 108.
- Verify candidate explanations against individual catalogue members, including
  source categories such as simpler bilinear realizations, Zobel
  transformations, Cauer-Foster relationships, regularity arguments, and related
  Y-delta cases.

## Done means

- The 108 comparison is supported by per-entry evidence rather than an
  unexplained stronger canonicalisation step.
- Star-delta effects are separated from other electrical-network transformations
  and realizability arguments.
- Any residual gap from the augmented structural classes to 108 is listed with
  concrete examples.
